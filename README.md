# Data Game Simulation
Name: Two Dice and a Deck
Inspiration: drop dead (dice game) and pig (dice game)
How to Play: Played with a group of 2+ players. On a player's turn they will roll two dice and draw 5 cards and they have the chance to earn points. They will replace the cards back into the deck and the next player will go. The player with more points wins. If it results in a tie, another round will be played. 
Rules:
if the sum of the two dice or the number on either of the individual dice matches one of the cards chosen the player earns no points. 
If the dice do not match the cards, they earn the amount of points of the sum of their two dice. 
Cards must be replaced after each player’s turn.
Jack = 11 Queen = 12 King = 0 Ace = 1

## Features: 
simulation for 1000 turns of the game
